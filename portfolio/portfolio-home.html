Hi! Welcome to my AP Computer Science Principles website :)

This summer, I attended the Summer Science Program (SSP) in Astrophysics at New Mexico Tech with 35 other high school students. At SSP, we determined the orbit of asteroids in teams of 3. Not only did we get to collect data on our asteroid, but we also got to take lots of pretty pictures! All the pictures below were taken at Etscorn Observatory in Socorro, NM by my fellow SSPers and me, and the photos of the nebulae were taken with the C-14 telescope. Sadly, the image quality of these photos is greatly reduced due to upload compression, but I hope you enjoy!!
